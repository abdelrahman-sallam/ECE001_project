It is also important to take the appropriate precautions to mitigate those threats

Some of those measures include

  • Hiring qualified personnel
  • Installing security hardware and software
  • Educating the users on proper mobile computing ethics
  • Auditing and developing sound, effective policies to govern mobile computing
  • Enforcing proper access rights and permissions

    • These are just a few ways to help deter any company's plans to offer mobile computing from possible threats. Since information is vital, all possible safeguard measures should be evaluated and implemented. Without such measures, it is possible to infiltrate exploits and other unknown threats, and cause irrefutable harm. There may be reputational or financial sanctions.
