Application of mobile computing

Some of the major field in which mobile computing can be applied are

  • Web or Internet access.
  • Global Position System(GPS).
  • Emergency services.
  • Entertainment services
  • Educational services.

MOBILE FINANCIAL APPLICATIONS

  1. Mobile Banking and Financial Services Customers can use their mobile handsets to access account balances, pay bills, and transfer funds using SMS
  2. Wireless Electronic Payment Systems Wireless payment systems transform mobile phones into secure, selfcontained purchasing support tools capable of instantly authorizing payments over the cellular network
  3. m-wallet (mobile wallet) Technologies that enable cardholders to make purchases with a single click from their wireless device
