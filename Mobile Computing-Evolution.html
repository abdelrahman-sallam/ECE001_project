Various technologies have emerged here. Those have grown around the world to support existing computer networks. With mobile computing we find that it has eradicated the need to be confined within one physical location. We hear about words like telecommuting, which can operate from home or from the field while at the same time accessing resources as though one were in the workplace. The introduction of portable computers and laptops, Personal Digital Assistants (PDA), PC tablets and smartphones has rendered mobile computing quite convenient in effect. The portability of these tools ensures and enables consumers to access all services as if they were in their company's internal network Tablet PC and iPads are used. This new technology allows users to update documents, surf the internet, send and receive emails, stream live video files, take photographs and also support video and voice conferencing. The constant and ever-increasing demand for superior and robust smart devices has been a catalyst for market share. Every manufacturer is trying to carve a niche on the market to itself. These devices are invented and innovated to deliver cutting-edge applications and services. For example , various cellular phone manufacturers have developed unique smartphones capable of performing the same task as computers and at the same processing speed. The market share is continuously being fought for for by different rivals. For example, Apple's iPhone OS manufacturers, Microsoft Windows Mobile Google's Android, Blackberry OS Research In Motion, are constantly competing to deliver better products with each update. The need for better, compact, affordable, and durable technology has made these vendors constantly creative. Market figures and statistics show an ever increasing need to buy and use these devices for professional or personal use It is in this light that the services are developed or innovated to suit long-term implementation. It has also prompted other vendors in the industry to adopt services that will provide better services. Cellular service providers, for example, are being pushed to develop and be creative in attracting more customers. This can be in terms of high-speed internet and data access, voice and video service etc. Hence the introduction of multiple types of networks such as 2 G, 2.5 G, 3 G, 4 G network infrastructure. The essence of mobile computing is the freedom to operate from anywhere. The use of iPads, tablets , smartphones, and notebooks has pushed these devices into demand. Modern day workers have such devices that they can perform their work from the confines of their own place. To access and store vast quantities of critical data, these tools are designed to. Executive and top management can make ready-made decisions without going to the office. Sales reports and market predictions, for example, can be accessed through these devices or a meeting can be held through video or audio conferencing via these devices With these features being high in demand, manufacturers are constantly coming up with applications that are geared towards supporting various mobile computing services. .
